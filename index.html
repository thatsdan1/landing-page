About Me

My name is Daniel Odetoye, and I am a rising sophomore at the University of Maryland, College Park, majoring in Computer Science and aspiring to be a software engineer. I am part of the Amazon Next, CodePath, the MLT Ascend Program, and the Quest Honors Program, where I have developed strong technical skills and a passion for innovation.

I also have a keen interest in UX design and graphic design, combining my technical skills with creativity to craft user-friendly and visually appealing solutions. Currently, I am actively seeking internship opportunities in software engineering roles to further grow in my tech journey.
