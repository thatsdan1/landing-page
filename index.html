About Me

Hi! I'm Daniel Odetoye, a Computer Science major at the University of Maryland, College Park, with a passion for innovation and an eagerness to learn. As a driven and adaptable individual, I’m committed to tackling challenges and seeing them as opportunities for growth. I’m an active participant in programs like Amazon Next, CodePath, MLT Ascend, and the Quest Honors Program, where I’ve developed technical skills and built a foundation for my career as a software engineer.
